My baby Kora

Mi little Kora is a Belgian Malinois who happens to be almost one year old. She came to me when she was only 2 months. She has grown pretty well, she's very energetic... everytime I see her my day lights up. I can't think about a future when she's not with me anymore.


She's afraid of toys with sharp sound, she absolutely hates when someone from outside tries to get close to our yard (sometimes she can't tell if I'm me or not, and just barks at my face until I speak up). She's always funny and often slips in the doorway... But the things that she likes the most are:

  • Chase birds
  • Bite people
  • Meat and chicken
  • Plastic bottles
  • To bite the water
